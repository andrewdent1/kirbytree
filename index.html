Expert Tree Services & Landscaping

Welcome to Kirby's Expert Tree Service

For over four decades, Kirby's Expert Tree Service has proudly provided professional tree care and landscaping services to homeowners and businesses in Flagler Beach and the surrounding areas. Founded in 1978, our company has deep roots in the community and a long-standing reputation for quality, safety, and reliability.

Our founder, Kim Kirby, built this business on hard work and a passion for enhancing natural spaces. Today, Kim co-owns and operates with his wife, Diana, continuing the legacy of professional service and expert care.

If you're looking to plant new saplings, remove dangerous tree limbs, or clear out old stumps, our team brings decades of hands-on experience and a deep understanding of Florida’s unique landscape. We specialize in everything from tree trimming and pruning to complete tree removal, always with a focus on safety, environmental responsibility, and your property's long-term health.

Talk with Kirby's Expert Tree Service to discuss your vision, and how our services can make your beautiful ideas a reality.
