Welcome to Kirby's Expert Tree Service

For over four decades, Kirby's Expert Tree Service has proudly provided professional tree care and landscaping services to homeowners and businesses in Flagler Beach and the surrounding areas. Founded in 1978, our company has deep roots in the community and a long-standing reputation for quality, safety, and reliability.

Our founder, Kim Kirby, built this business on hard work and a passion for enhancing natural spaces. Today, Kim co-owns and operates the company with his wife, Diana, continuing the legacy of professional service and expert care.

Whether you're planting saplings, removing hazardous limbs, or clearing old stumps, our team brings decades of hands-on experience and a deep understanding of Florida’s unique landscape. We specialize in tree trimming, pruning, and complete removals—always with a focus on safety, environmental responsibility, and the long-term health of your property.

Contact Kirby's Expert Tree Service today to share your vision and discover how we can bring your outdoor space to life.
